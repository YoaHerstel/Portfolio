The 'Bored Girl' Project

I've always been fascinated by the latest technologies and innovations, so when I stumbled upon the world of NFTs during my travels, I was immediately hooked. As an artist, I saw NFTs as the perfect way to showcase my creativity and reach a wider audience.

To create my NFTs, I first drew all the layers separately on ProCreate. Then by turning on and off each layer and changing the colours, I crafted 1000 unique images by hand. In the meantime, I created a Twitter account to got involved in the NFT community, and quickly realized that I needed a Discord server as well. With the help of a friend, I got everything set up and even organized some fun activities to engage the community. It was so awesome to see the enthusiasm and support pouring in from all sides. In fact, we gained over 2000 followers on Twitter alone!

Overall, this project was a blast to work on and taught me so much about NFTs, community building, and the power of creativity. I'm incredibly proud of what we accomplished and can't wait to take this or my new NFT project to the next level!
